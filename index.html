Hello, I'm a Physics Ph.D. Student at The Ohio State University! I'm working on particle-in-cell simulations and applying machine learning methods to optimize laser-induced ion acceleration in Dr. Christopher Orban's high energy density physics (HEDP) group. As someone who has studied not only physics, but mathematics and computer science, I have a diverse skillset in quantitative/statistical methods, programming languages, and physical intuitition that has been honed over the course of my graduate degree. Links to my GitHub, Google Scholar, LinkedIn, email and more can be found by clicking the corresponding icon at the bottom of the page!
